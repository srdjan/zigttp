# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

The zttp project is committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free
experience for everyone who participates, regardless of background or identity.

## Standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of
conduct. The full text - the expected standards of behavior, the
responsibilities of maintainers, and the enforcement guidelines - is published
at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in zttp you agree to uphold that standard in all project
spaces (the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews) and
whenever you are representing the project in a public space.

## Enforcement

Concerns about participant conduct may be reported privately to the project
maintainers by contacting the repository owner, **@srdjan**, through GitHub.
All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and security of anyone who reports an
incident.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may
face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by the project's
leadership.
